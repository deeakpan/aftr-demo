# AFTRMarket

AFTRMarket is a prediction market app focused on fast market participation and clear outcome ownership.

Users connect a wallet, explore live market categories, and take positions on outcomes. Each position is represented by transferable outcome shares that can be held, traded, or redeemed after settlement.

## What Users Can Do

- Connect a wallet and sign in with a wallet signature.
- Set a display name to participate in market activity.
- Browse markets by category (Trending, Newest, Crypto, Politics, Finance, Tech, Economy, Sports, Gaming, Breaking).
- View available balance and prepare deposits.
- Navigate market features quickly on desktop and mobile.

## How AFTRMarket Works (User View)

- Markets are created with clear outcomes and an expiry time.
- You choose an outcome and deposit collateral to receive outcome shares.
- If your outcome wins at settlement, your shares can be redeemed for payout.
- Shares are transferable, so users can exit or reposition before final settlement.

## Settlement Types

AFTRMarket supports two market styles:

- Event markets: settle from verified real-world outcomes.
- Price markets: settle from asset price conditions at expiry.

## Design Goals

- Clear and simple participation flow.
- Mobile-friendly navigation with fast access to key actions.
- Transparent outcome and redemption behavior.
- Fair market mechanics that avoid hidden complexity for users.
